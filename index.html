What do we do to bring smiles?

Emotional Support & Counseling: Provide counseling sessions to address the emotional and psychological needs of orphaned children, promoting mental well-being and resilience

Creative Arts and Talent Development: Offer activities such as art, music, dance, and drama to help children explore and develop their creative abilities.

Mentorship Initiatives: Connect children with mentors who guide them in personal development, life skills, and career advice as they prepare for adulthood.

Sports and Recreational Activities: Organize sports events and physical activities to promote teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness among the children.

About Discoveries with Fey

Founded in 2024 by Feizun Khaoma, Discoveries with Fey is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering orphaned and vulnerable children by providing them with educational, emotional, and social support. Our mission is to offer holistic care that nurtures not only the intellectual growth of the children but also their emotional well-being and life skills.

At Discoveries with Fey, we believe that every child, regardless of their background, deserves the opportunity to discover their full potential. Through our programs, we aim to create an environment where children can thrive, grow in confidence, and become self-sufficient contributors to society.

We partner with communities, educators, and volunteers to offer educational workshops, counseling, mentorship, and extracurricular activities, all tailored to the needs of the children in orphanages across the country

Our vision is to create a world where every orphaned and vulnerable child has access to the resources and opportunities needed to thrive, achieve their dreams, and lead fulfilling lives. We aspire to break the cycle of poverty and empower children to become confident, self-reliant individuals who make meaningful contributions to their communities and society. By fostering a nurturing environment filled with learning, love, and support, we aim to inspire a future where no child is left behind.

Our mission at Discoveries with Fey is to empower orphaned and vulnerable children by providing them with the tools, support, and opportunities to discover their potential. Through education, mentorship, emotional support, and life skills training, we aim to nurture a generation of resilient, confident, and capable individuals who are prepared to overcome life's challenges and contribute positively to society. We believe in creating a safe, inclusive, and loving environment where every child feels valued and inspired to pursue their dreams.
